Library Borrowing Guidelines

Please read the following rules before borrowing any library material.

Eligibility

  • Only active registered members may borrow books.
  • Membership must remain active throughout the borrowing period.

Borrow Limit

  • Maximum books allowed at one time: {{ borrow_limit }}
  • A new book cannot be issued after reaching the limit.

Loan Period

  • Each issued book must be returned within {{ loan_days }} days.
  • The due date is calculated automatically during issue.

Late Return & Fine

  • Late returns attract a daily fine.
  • Fine starts after the due date.
  • The longer the delay, the higher the fine rate.
  • The fine will never exceed the purchase price of the book plus ₹100.

Damaged or Lost Books

  • Members are responsible for books issued to them.
  • Damaged or lost books may require replacement charges.
  • The librarian's decision regarding compensation is final.

General Rules

  • Handle books carefully.
  • Do not write, highlight or tear pages.
  • Return books to the circulation desk only.
  • Keep your contact information updated.
