Library Borrowing Guidelines

Please read the borrowing rules and fine policy before issuing any library material.

Eligibility

  • Only active registered members may borrow books.
  • Membership must remain active throughout the borrowing period.

Borrow Limit

  • Maximum books allowed at one time: {{ borrow_limit }}
  • No additional books can be issued after reaching the borrowing limit.

Loan Period

  • Every issued book is borrowed for {{ loan_days }} days.
  • The due date is generated automatically during issue.
  • Books should be returned on or before the due date.

Late Return

  • No fine is charged during the loan period.
  • Fine starts from the day after the due date.
  • The daily fine increases every 30 overdue days.
  • Refer to the Fine Schedule below.

Damaged or Lost Books

  • Members are responsible for books issued to them.
  • Damaged or lost books may require replacement charges.
  • The librarian's decision regarding compensation is final.

General Rules

  • Handle library books carefully.
  • Do not write, underline or tear pages.
  • Return books only through the circulation desk.
  • Keep your contact details updated.

Fine Schedule

Overdue Period Fine Charged
Within {{ loan_days }} days No Fine
Days 1 – 30 overdue ₹5 per day
Days 31 – 60 overdue ₹10 per day
Days 61 – 90 overdue ₹15 per day
Every additional 30 overdue days Daily fine increases by ₹5
Maximum Fine Purchase Price of the Book + ₹100

Important Notice

• Members with unpaid fines may not be allowed to borrow additional books.
• Members who have already reached the borrowing limit cannot issue another book until one is returned.
• Fine calculations are performed automatically by the Library Management System.
